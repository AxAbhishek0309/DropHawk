All that you need to know about Business Case Study Presentation Challenge

Business Case Study Presentation Challenge
 
Task Overview: Participate in the Business Case Study Presentation Challenge by selecting an industry of your choice and creating a case study presentation using Analytx charts and dataset (https://analytx.in). Record a video presentation (1-3 minutes) showcasing your case study, which will be uploaded to YouTube. Topics can include examples like "How Maruti saved XYZ amount of money using this approach" or "How Reliance is fighting the cold drinks war with Coca-Cola and winning?"
 
Challenge Details:
  • Industry Selection: Choose any industry of interest.
  • Presentation: Create and present a video (minimum 1 minute, maximum 3 minutes; videos exceeding 3 minutes will be disqualified).

JOIN the WhatsApp Group: Link

Rounds:

  • Round 1 - Internal Evaluation: Submit your edited video.
  • Round 2 - Public Evaluation: Uploaded videos on YouTube will be judged based on the highest number of likes plus unique comments.
Rules and Guidelines:
  • Video Length: Videos must be between 1 and 3 minutes. Submissions outside this range will be disqualified.
  • Exclusivity: Use only charts and dataset from Analytx (you can use external dataset for context research); no external datasets are allowed.
  • Content Originality: All content must be original; plagiarism will result in immediate disqualification.
  • Deadline: Round 1 submissions are due by 11:59 PM IST on August 1, 2025. Round 2 voting ends on August 3, 2025, at 11:59 PM IST 
  • Language: Presentations must be in English.
  • Public Engagement: Encourage unique comments and likes on YouTube videos during Round 2, but avoid artificial inflation (e.g., bots), which will lead to disqualification.

Important dates & deadlines?

  • Registration Deadline 01 Aug 25, 12:00 AM IST
